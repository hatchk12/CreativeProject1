Origami Puffy Star


step 1: find a long strip of paper

step 2: tie a knot near the end leaving a little tail

step 3: fold the tail back and tuck it in

step 4: fold the long strip around the knot

step 5: keep folding and the paper will fall into place

step 6: take the exta bit and tuck it in

step 7: push in all the sides to make the star pop up

All Done!
