Origami Grasshopper


step 1: find a square piece of paper

step 2: fold diagonally, creating a triangle

step 3: fold the triangle in half again

step 4: fold the top half back to make another triangle

step 5: fold the bottom corner up to meet the edge

step 6: repeat steps 4-5 on the back half

step 7: tap the head or tail to make it jump

Good Job!
